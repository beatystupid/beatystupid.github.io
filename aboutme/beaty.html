Hello! By being on this page, I assume you want
to learn about what a Beaty is. A Beaty is a
fictional character that I made. I use it in
names for games, I use it as a logo, I draw it
way too much, and I tell my friends about it all
the time. If you go to the Beaty Homepage,
then click “Beaty,” it displays an image of a
Beaty. I’ve made a bunch of other characters,
but the Beaty is easily my favorite, and I don’t
know why. But enough about Beaties. Click
another tab to learn about something else.
